The Expectation Reality Gap Analysis:

Executive Expectations: IBM data shows CEOs expect 49% of organizations to be driving growth with AI by 2026, with significant productivity gains expected within 18 months.

Current Reality: DataArt's research shows only 11% of pilots reach production, with 73% citing governance issues. If current trends continue, only 30% might achieve meaningful scaling by 2026.

The Bridge: Accenture's front-runner model suggests the gap is bridgeable for organizations that invest in foundational capabilities and governance-first approaches.
